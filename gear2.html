Monkey D. Luffy

Gear Second

1. Name/Title: Gear Second (Gia Sekando)
2. What It Is: Luffy uses his rubber body as a pump to accelerate his blood flow.
3. First Appearance: Chapter 387 (manga) and Episode 272 (anime)
4. Abilities/Powers: Exponentially increases speed, enhances strength and attack power, improves reflexes and reaction time.
5. Signature Moves: Jet Pistol, Jet Gatling, Jet Bazooka, Jet Whip, Jet Bullet, Jet Rifle, and Jet Stamp.
6. Weaknesses/Drawbacks: Initially caused severe bodily strain, shortened lifespan with each use, and led to extreme exhaustion after deactivation; required rest period between uses.
