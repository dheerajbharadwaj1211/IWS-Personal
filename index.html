Monkey D. Luffy

Base Form

Who is Luffy?
Luffy is a young pirate captain who dreams of becoming the Pirate King. He leads a crew called the Straw Hat Pirates.
What are Devil Fruits?
Devil Fruits are special magical fruits in the One Piece world. When someone eats one, they gain a unique superpower.
About the Gomu Gomu no Mi:
When Luffy was a kid, he accidentally ate a Devil Fruit that turned his entire body into rubber.
Base Form Abilities:
Luffy can stretch his arms and legs incredibly far to punch enemies from a distance. He can also inflate himself like a balloon and use his elasticity to bounce around or dodge attacks.
Evolution Path:
As Luffy faces stronger enemies, he develops new techniques called "Gears" that power up his rubber abilities. Each Gear makes him faster, stronger, or gives him new ways to fight, helping him take on tougher opponents.
