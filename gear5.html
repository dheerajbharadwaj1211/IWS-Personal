Monkey D. Luffy

Gear Fifth

1. Name/Title: Gear Fifth (Gia Fifusu)
2. What It Is: The awakened form of Luffy's true Devil Fruit, the Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika. This transformation grants Luffy the properties of the mythical Sun God Nika.
3. First Appearance: Chapter 1044 (manga) and Episode 1071 (anime)
4. Abilities/Powers: Complete freedom of movement and imagination; can turn the environment into rubber; defies conventional physics with cartoon like abilities; grants enhanced strength, speed, and durability far beyond previous forms; can grab and manipulate lightning.
5. Signature Moves: Gomu Gomu no Bajrang Gun (giant fist attack), Gomu Gomu no Gigant, Dawn Cymbal, Mogura Pistol, and various improvised cartoon like attacks limited only by imagination.
6. Weaknesses/Drawbacks: Rapidly drains stamina and energy, initially causes Luffy to age rapidly when the form ends, requires intense concentration and willpower to maintain, and if Luffy's heart stops beating with the "Drums of Liberation" rhythm, the form deactivates.
